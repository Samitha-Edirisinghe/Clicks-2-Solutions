Pawning Centers Management System

Pawning Centers Management System


System will keep all details of Pawning,sales and purchasing, Stocks, Customers, Suppliers, Income /Expenses and Ledger Accounting, Final Accounting and all activities of the company as well as handle the online Billing system. It proposes many new facilities aim at providing better service to your customers and a better working environment for your company management activities.

Modules of the System

  1. Centralize Client Database (All Branch Clients in a One Database)
  2. Client History
    • Pending Loans
    • Payment History
    • Client Type (Regular / Blacklisted /etc)
  3. Article Details
    • Article Type
    • Gold Type and Rate
    • Market Value / Payable Amount
  4. Loan Type
    • Loan Period
    • Interest Rate
  5. Pawning
    • Online billing
    • Check the client history
  6. Redemption
    • Calculate Interest
    • Facility to provide the discount
  7. Part Payment
      Interest Payment Payment from Capital
  8. Reminders
  9. List of to be Forfeited Bills
  10. Bill Forfeiting
  11. Stock Controlling
    • Stock in from the Pawning with Tag No
    • Stock out when bill Redemption
    • Stock Report
      • Item wise (Chain / Pendent/etc) with Weight and Value
      • Bill wise with Wight and Value
      • Loan Type wise with weight and Value
  12. Bill Auction
  13. Reports
    • Administrational Requirements (On Line, Daily, Monthly, Periodically, Annually, requirement summaries etc. )
    • Approval with closures, Disbursements, Standard Documents, Notices etc.
    • Customer ( Receipts, Status ,Reminders, Summary, etc.)
